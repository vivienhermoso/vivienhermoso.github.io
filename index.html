About Me

Vivien Ericca I. Hermoso recently graduated with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, magna cum laude, from Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna in October 2024. She has focused on front-end and mobile development, with a keen interest in software Quality Assurance (QA).

Vivien gained significant experience in mobile development through her work on key projects, including iOS development for Mapúa MCL's digital passport scanner for iPad and Android development for e-ganapp.

She has consistently demonstrated academic excellence as a Dean’s Lister and was recognized as a President’s Lister for A.Y. 2022-2023. Vivien has served as the Publication Committee Chair of Mapúa MCL-ACM, Vice President for Audit and Internal Relations Associate of Mapúa MCL-SSC, 4th Year Representative of JPCS Mapúa MCL, and SSC Representative of Mapúa MCL-CCIS SC.

Through her academic and extracurricular efforts, Vivien has shown a strong commitment to her field and community.
